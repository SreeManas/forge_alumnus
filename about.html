Our Story

Every Voice Has a Story

Talkiepedia is a career knowledge platform by Forge Alumnus. We bring authentic, thought-provoking conversations with industry leaders to help you navigate your professional journey — from campus to corporate.

Our Mission

We believe career knowledge shouldn't be locked behind expensive courses or exclusive networks. Talkiepedia democratizes access to industry insights by capturing real conversations with professionals from Microsoft, Collins Aerospace, Deloitte, Amazon, and more.

Every episode is more than a podcast — it's a career compass. Our guests share the unfiltered truths about their industries, the mistakes they made, the lessons they learned, and the advice they wish they'd received.

Our Vision

To become the most accessible career guidance platform for students and early-career professionals in India — powered by authentic stories, structured learning paths, and AI-driven knowledge discovery.

Our Journey

How We Got Here

2023

The Idea Takes Shape

The team at Forge Alumnus realized that students lacked accessible career guidance from real industry professionals. Talkiepedia was born as a podcast to bridge this gap.

2024

First Episodes Go Live

We launched with conversations featuring professionals from Microsoft, Collins Aerospace, and Deloitte. The response was overwhelming — students wanted more.

2025

Growing the Community

100+ subscribers, 20+ episodes, guests from 6 major companies. Talkiepedia evolved from a podcast into a career knowledge platform.

2026

AI-Powered Knowledge Discovery

The redesign introduces Talkie AI, learning paths, and searchable transcripts — transforming Talkiepedia from a video gallery into an intelligent career companion.
